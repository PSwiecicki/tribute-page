Steve Jobs

Genius, visionary and inventor.

About Steve Jobs

1955

Born in San Francisco, California.

1972

Finishes high school and starts studying on Reed College in Portland, Oregon which he leaves the same year.

1974

Returns to California and starts working in Atari.

1975

Meets Wozniak in "Homebrew Computer Club".

1976

Creates the first Apple computer.

1984

Macintosh computer goes on sale.

1985

Resigns from Apple.

1986

Starts a new computer company - Next Inc. and the same year buys Pixar.

1989

First Next computer goes on sale.

1997

Comes back to Apple as an "interim" CEO.

2001

The first iPod goes on sale.

2004

Undergoes surgery for a pancreatic cancer.

2007

Apple releases it's first iPhone.

2011

Dies at the age of 56.
